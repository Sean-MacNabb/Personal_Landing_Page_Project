About Me

I am an aspiring full-stack developer with a strong interest in building thoughtful, useful, and scalable software. After earning my BS in IT, I began focusing more seriously on software development and found that I am most energized by complex problems, continuous learning, and the process of turning ideas into working solutions. I enjoy both frontend and backend development, and I am especially interested in the future of AI, automation, and agentic systems.

What I Bring

Hospitality to Tech

Before moving into tech, I spent more than 10 years in fine dining hospitality. That experience shaped my communication style, professionalism, attention to detail, and ability to work well with people in fast-paced environments. It continues to influence how I approach development, not just as someone who can build, but as someone who can collaborate, communicate clearly, and create positive experiences.

My current focus is growing as a software developer while continuing to build toward full-stack engineering and AI-driven work. I am especially interested in opportunities that will help me gain experience, grow through mentorship, and contribute to projects involving intelligent systems, automation, and practical problem solving.

How I Work

Collaboration

I do my best work when I am learning and building alongside other people, especially in environments where ideas can be shared openly and momentum is visible.

Growth

I am early in my development career, so I value mentorship, curiosity, and hands-on problem solving that helps me keep sharpening both skill and confidence.

Energy

I feed off shared effort and progress. Seeing work come together through a team makes the process more exciting and pushes me to contribute at a higher level.

Beyond the Code

Outside of development, I enjoy spending time with my dog, my family, and my friends. I also stay creative through music, sports, reading, cooking, screenwriting, and acting, all of which help shape the energy and perspective I bring into my work.
